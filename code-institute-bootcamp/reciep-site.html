How to make cake

Everyone loves cake! Lets get started with my favourite recipe for cupcakes so that we can decorate them with intricate icing patterns later!

ingredients

  • sugar
  • butter
  • self raising flour
  • eggs
  • cocoa powder (optional)

preparation

  1. cream together the sugar and the butter till is is a smooth paste.
  2. If you are using cocoa powder, mix this into your flour base now, if not skip this step.
  3. next add in 1/3 of the flour and 1 egg, mix until everything is combined.
  4. repeat this step 2 more times untill the whole mix is combined.
  5. spoon the mix into prelined cupccaes cases and bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees.
  6. check the cakes are cooked by inserting a skewer into the middle of the cake, if it comes out clean, they are done.
  7. take out of the oven and leave to cool.
  8. Enjoy with a cup of tea, or see the next recipe for icing ideas!
